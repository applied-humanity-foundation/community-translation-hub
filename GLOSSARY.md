## Contributing to the Glossary

When you encounter a technical term that isn't in this glossary:

1. Check if there's an established translation used by major Chinese tech companies
2. Prefer translations used by official Microsoft/Apple/Google Chinese localizations
3. If no established translation exists, keep the English term and add a brief Chinese explanation
4. Submit a PR to add the term to this glossary
